# Copy Rules

Apply these rules to every generated review caption or reusable text package.

## Disallowed Patterns

- Do not add hashtags
- Do not use the `not ... but ...` sentence pattern
- Do not use `—`
- Do not use the phrase `more than just`
- Do not overuse neat parallelism such as `Faster. Smarter. Scalable. Secure.`

## Tone

- Prefer a straightforward testimonial framing
- Keep the caption grounded in the actual review text
- Avoid ad-like phrasing
